Getting Started
How to Use
This Journal
📖
Learn
Before each session, read the concept in the MAJ app. It is short — 2 to 3 minutes. Every concept makes your practice smarter. Mark it as read to earn your XP.
⚡
Do
Complete the session challenge with your coach. This is the most important part. Skill is built in the real world, not on a screen. Show up and do the work.
✏️
Reflect
After every session, write your reflection in this journal. Minimum 5 words — but aim for 30 or more. The athletes who reflect grow faster than those who don't. Your journal is your proof of growth.
🏅
Earn Your Badges
On badge weeks your coach assesses your performance and awards your badge in the app. When you earn it — your coach will also give you a gold star sticker for your Quest Map. Place it with pride.
The rule: Complete all three steps — Learn, Do, Reflect — to unlock the next week and earn your full XP. No shortcuts. Every step counts.
