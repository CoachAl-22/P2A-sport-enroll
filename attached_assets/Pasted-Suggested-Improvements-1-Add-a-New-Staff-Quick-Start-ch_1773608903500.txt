Suggested Improvements
1. Add a "New Staff Quick Start" checklist at the very beginning
Before Section 1, coaches need a simple "Day 1" list:

Log in at power2adapt.online
Check your assigned classes (Attendance page)
Read the Coach Reference Guide (/onboarding)
Bookmark the attendance page on your phone
Young coaches (18-25) will skip to this first -- walls of text won't get read.

2. Add a "Before & After Session" checklist for coaches
The most practical thing a coach needs daily isn't covered:

Check class roster before leaving
Mark attendance within 1 hour of session ending
Any injury/incident -- record in athlete notes immediately
3. PDF rendering artifacts to fix
A few sections have broken formatting from the HTML-to-PDF conversion:

Section 2.4 Step 1 -- "From the dashboard, click Find New Programs" renders with a mid-sentence line break
Section 2.5 Step 1 -- "the parent is taken to the Checkout page" has a split layout
Section 4.6 Step 3 -- "number of teaching weeks" breaks mid-phrase
These read fine in the HTML version but look odd in the PDF. Worth either fixing the PDF styling or telling staff to use the live HTML version at /operations-manual instead of the PDF.

4. Incomplete "Key Contacts" table (last page)
The table only shows admin login emails -- no coach-specific contacts. Add:

Alistair's mobile for urgent issues
Who to call if the site is down
Who handles parent payment disputes
5. Setmore not mentioned
Coaches may receive class-related information from Setmore (booking confirmations, athlete lists). Worth a brief note that Setmore is the booking system and syncs to Google Calendar -- so they know what it is if they see it.

6. Consider a role-specific one-pager
The full 20 pages is comprehensive for admin but a lot for a Level 1 coach who just needs attendance and video highlights. A single-page "Coach Quick Reference" pulling out Sections 3.1, 3.2, and the page URL table would be worth adding as an appendix.

Priority Order
Fix PDF rendering artifacts (quick)
Add New Staff Quick Start checklist (high value, 5 mins)
Before/After Session checklist (high value for coaches)
Complete the Key Contacts table (important for escalation)
Role-specific one-pager (nice to have)
Overall it's ready to go once you've verified the functionality. The HTML version at /operations-manual is cleaner than the PDF -- I'd direct staff to that as the primary reference.
