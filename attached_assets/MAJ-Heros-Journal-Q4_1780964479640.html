Q4 · WEEK 4
Handling Mistakes
Your response to a mistake is more important than the mistake itself. A reset cue is a physical or verbal action that signals to your brain: that is done, I am moving on. Accountability without self-punishment: acknowledge, identify what to do differently, move on.

Giving and Receiving Feedback
Effective feedback: specific, timely, focused on the action not the person. Feedback sandwich: one positive + one specific improvement + one encouraging close. Asking for feedback is strength, not weakness.
