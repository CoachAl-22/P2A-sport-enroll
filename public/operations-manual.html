New Staff — Start Here

🎯 New Staff Quick Start

Complete these steps on your first day. Takes about 10 minutes — then you're ready to coach.

  • Log in at www.power2adapt.online using the credentials provided by your admin.
  • Go to power2adapt.online/attendance — confirm your assigned classes appear in the dropdown.
  • Read the Coach Reference Guide (power2adapt.online/onboarding) — covers session protocols, red flags, and communication standards.
  • Bookmark the Attendance page on your phone — you'll need it at every session.
  • Note: Setmore is used for Running Tech, Team Sport Speed, Senior Squad, and High Performance bookings. You may receive Setmore booking confirmations by email — these sync to Google Calendar. If you're unsure, ask your admin.
  • Save the Key Contacts from Section 6 of this manual in case you ever need urgent support.
Section 1

Platform Overview

Power2ADAPT is a full-featured athletic program management system. It handles class discovery, enrollment, payment, athlete tracking, and communications — all in one place accessible from any device.

The platform lives at www.power2adapt.online and is available 24/7. No app download is required — it works in any web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

User Roles

Every account has one of three roles that controls what they can see and do:

RoleWho uses itAccess level
ParentFamilies with enrolled childrenFamily dashboard, enrolment, payments, video viewing, questionnaires
CoachTraining staffAttendance marking, video highlights, athlete assessments, onboarding guide
AdminBusiness owner / senior staffEverything — full system access including all admin pages

Logging In

  1. Go to www.power2adapt.online and click Login in the top navigation bar.
  2. Enter your email address (or mobile number or User ID) and your password.
  3. Click Sign In. You will be taken directly to your dashboard.
ℹ️
Admin login credentials are kept separately by the business owner. If a staff member needs an account created, see Section 4.2 — Managing Staff Accounts.
💡
Tip: You can log in with your email address, Australian mobile number, or your assigned User ID — whichever is easiest to remember.

2.2 Registration & Login

Parents create their own account directly on the website. Staff do not need to create parent accounts manually.

  1. Click Get Started or Login → Create Account on the homepage.
  2. Fill in first name, last name, email address, mobile number, and a password.
  3. Australian mobile numbers are validated automatically (must start with 04XX or be a valid landline — +61 prefix also accepted).
  4. Account is created immediately — no email verification required.

2.3 Family Dashboard

After logging in, parents land on their Family Dashboard at power2adapt.online/

The dashboard shows:

  • Current Programs — all active enrolments with status badges (Active, Pending Payment, Waitlisted)
  • Quick Actions — Find New Programs, Weekly Schedule, Payments & Billing
  • Notifications — system alerts such as payment confirmations and waitlist updates
  • Invoice Actions — view and download invoices for completed payments

2.4 Enrolling in a Program

  1. From the dashboard, click Find New Programs, or go to power2adapt.online/classes.
  2. Browse available classes filtered by age group, day, or venue. Each card shows capacity, times, venue, coach, and price.
  3. Click Enrol Now on the chosen class.
  4. Select the child to enrol (or add a new child profile — name, date of birth, grade, any medical notes).
  5. Review the enrolment summary and confirm. The enrolment is created with status Pending Payment.
  6. Proceed to checkout to complete payment (see Section 2.5).
⚠️
If a class is full, the Enrol Now button becomes Join Waitlist. The parent is placed on the waitlist and notified by SMS when a spot opens.

2.5 Making a Payment

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Power2ADAPT does not store card details.

  1. After enrolling, the parent is taken to the Checkout page automatically, or can return to it from the dashboard.
  2. Enter card details (card number, expiry, CVC) in the Stripe payment form.
  3. Click Pay Now. Payment is processed in real time.
  4. On success, enrolment status changes to Active, an SMS confirmation is sent, and an email receipt is generated.
  5. Invoices are available to download from the dashboard at any time.
ℹ️
Pricing is calculated automatically based on the term's price-per-week setting and the number of teaching weeks (after public holidays are subtracted). Parents always see the exact amount before paying.

2.6 Joining a Waitlist

  1. When a class is at full capacity, click Join Waitlist on the class card.
  2. The parent's details are saved and they receive an SMS acknowledgement.
  3. When a spot becomes available, they are notified automatically by SMS to complete enrolment.
  4. Waitlist entries are visible on the parent dashboard.

2.7 Athlete Check-In Survey

The Running Improvement Check-In is an 18-question survey that helps coaches understand each athlete's goals and areas for improvement.

  • Available at power2adapt.online/questionnaire — no login required.
  • Covers: event focus, goals, training frequency, technique issues, mental approach, injury history, and more.
  • Responses are saved to the database and automatically synced to a Google Spreadsheet for coach review.
  • Admins can view all responses at power2adapt.online/admin/surveys.

2.8 Viewing Performance Videos

Coaches can share performance highlight videos with parents. Parents receive a link via SMS or email and can view the video on any device — no login required for shared links.

  • Shared videos are accessed at a unique private link e.g. power2adapt.online/video-highlights/[code]
  • Logged-in parents can also view their child's videos from the Athlete Portal.

2.9 Premium Program Applications

Two elite programs require a formal application rather than direct enrolment:

Senior Squad

For advanced athletes. Parents submit a detailed application form at power2adapt.online/senior-squad. Includes event details, PBs, training history, and goals.

High Performance Squad

Elite-level program. Applications submitted at power2adapt.online/high-performance. Admin reviews and approves/rejects via the admin panel.

On submission, the applicant receives an SMS confirmation and the admin team receives an SMS + email alert. Admins review applications at power2adapt.online/admin.

2.10 Education Hub

The Education Hub (formerly "Blog") lives at power2adapt.online/education.

  • Accessible to anyone — no login required.
  • Contains articles on training tips, technique guidance, and athlete development.
  • Articles link through to the Skool community for deeper discussion.
  • Admins publish and manage articles from power2adapt.online/admin/blog.

2.11 Contacting Power2ADAPT

Parents can submit an enquiry using the contact form on the homepage. They choose their preferred contact method (phone, email, or video call) and describe their enquiry. On submission:

  • The parent receives an email confirmation with a branded HTML receipt.
  • The admin team receives an SMS alert and an email notification with the enquiry details.
  • The enquiry is logged in the admin panel for follow-up tracking.
Section 3

Coach Functions

Coaches have access to a set of tools designed for day-to-day training operations. These pages are only visible after logging in with a coach or admin account.

3.1 Recording Attendance

Page: power2adapt.online/attendance  Coach Admin

  1. Log in as a coach or admin and navigate to Attendance in the top menu.
  2. Select the class from the dropdown (only classes assigned to that coach appear).
  3. Select the date (defaults to today).
  4. The roster of enrolled students loads automatically.
  5. For each student, click one of three status buttons: Present ✓, Absent ✗, or Late ⏱.
  6. For absent students, an optional reason field appears (e.g. illness, family commitment).
  7. Each mark is saved instantly — no "Submit" button needed.
💡
Today's classes appear highlighted at the top. You can also select any past date to update or review historical attendance records.

📋 Before & After Session Checklist

Use this as your daily session routine — print and keep it in your coaching bag if helpful.

⏰ Before the Session

  • Log in and open the Attendance page for today's class.
  • Check the class roster — note any medical alerts or new athletes.
  • Confirm the venue and start time (check Setmore if it's a Running Tech, Team Sport Speed, Senior Squad, or High Performance session).
  • Review any outstanding athlete goals or assessment notes if relevant.

✅ After the Session

  • Mark attendance within 1 hour of the session ending — select Present, Absent, or Late for each athlete.
  • Record any injury or incident in the athlete's notes immediately (Athlete Performance Manager → Tab 3).
  • If a video highlight was captured, upload it to Video Highlights and share with the relevant parent.
  • Update any performance records or goal progress while the session is fresh.

3.2 Performance Video Highlights

Page: power2adapt.online/video-highlights  Coach Admin

Coaches can upload, manage, and share performance video highlights with parents and the Skool community.

Adding a new video highlight:

  1. Click + Add Video.
  2. Fill in: Title, Description, Video Type (individual performance, class highlights, skill demonstration, progress comparison, team performance).
  3. Select the athlete (child) and/or the class the video relates to.
  4. Paste the video URL (e.g. YouTube or Vimeo link) and optional thumbnail URL.
  5. Add performance notes, coach comments, skills highlighted, and tags.
  6. Toggle Make Public if the video should be shareable without login.
  7. Click Save.

Sharing a video with a parent:

  1. Find the video in the list and click the Share icon.
  2. Select the parent's account from the dropdown, or enter an email address directly.
  3. Optionally add a personal message.
  4. Click Send — the parent receives an SMS with the video link and an optional email notification.

One-click share to Skool:

  1. Click the Skool button on any video card.
  2. The video details are pre-filled — add any additional community message.
  3. Confirm to post the highlight to the Skool community platform.

3.3 Athlete Performance & Assessments

Page: power2adapt.online/admin/athletes  Coach Admin

The Athlete Performance Manager has three tabs for each athlete:

Tab 1 — Performance Records

  • Log timed or measured results for 16 event types: 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 1500m, Long Jump, High Jump, Triple Jump, Shot Put, Discus, Javelin, Hurdles, Relay, Vertical Jump, Agility Test, Beep Test.
  • Each record has: event type, value, unit, date, notes, and a Personal Best flag (shown with a trophy icon).
  • Records can be edited or deleted at any time.

Tab 2 — Training Goals

  • Set SMART goals with: title, description, target value and unit, current value, target date, status (Active / Achieved / Paused), priority (Low / Medium / High), and category (Speed, Strength, Technique, Endurance, Flexibility, Agility).
  • A progress bar automatically calculates percentage toward the target.
  • Goals update to Achieved status with a timestamp when marked complete.

Tab 3 — Assessments & Feedback

  • Add coach notes, written feedback, or formal assessments with a title, entry type (Coach Note / Feedback / Formal Assessment / File Document), and free-text content.
  • Optionally attach a file (PDF, Word document, or image up to 10MB) — stored securely in cloud storage.
  • Each entry shows the author's name and date.
  • Attached files can be downloaded at any time via the file link.

Adding a new athlete to the system:

  1. Click the + Add button in the athlete sidebar.
  2. Enter first name, last name, date of birth, year level, emergency contact, medical notes, and optionally link to an existing parent account.
  3. Click Add Athlete — the athlete appears in the sidebar immediately.

The admin dashboard shows a live snapshot of the business:

  • Key metrics: Total users, active enrolments, revenue this term, pending payments

  • Quick links to all admin sections
  • Data tools: CSV import for bulk customer data, sample data generation for testing

4.2 Managing Classes

Page: power2adapt.online/admin/classes  Admin

Creating a new class:

  1. Click + Add Class.
  2. Fill in: Class name, description, sport type, venue, assigned coach, term, year, day of week, start time, end time, minimum age, maximum age, maximum capacity, and price per term.
  3. Click Create Class. The class immediately appears in the public classes page.

Editing an existing class:

  1. Find the class in the table and click the Edit (pencil) icon.
  2. Update any fields — all existing enrolments remain unaffected.
  3. Click Save Changes.

Deleting a class:

  1. Click the Delete (bin) icon on the class row.
  2. Confirm deletion. Note: this should only be done for classes with no active enrolments.
⚠️
Before creating a class, ensure the venue, coach, and term configuration already exist in the system. These are managed in Sections 4.3 and 4.5.

4.3 Managing Staff Accounts

Page: power2adapt.online/admin/staff  Admin

Creating a new staff account:

  1. Click + Add Staff Member.
  2. Enter: first name, last name, email address, mobile number, role (Coach, Admin, or Parent), temporary password, bio, and sport specialisations (can select multiple).
  3. Click Create Staff Member. They can now log in with the provided credentials.

Specialisation options include: Foundation (Prep–Year 2), Emerging (Year 3–6), Academy (Year 7+), Senior Squad, Empowered Athlete Program, Team Sport Speed, Basketball, Soccer, Tennis, Swimming, Athletics, Netball, Cricket, Volleyball, Multi-Sport.

Editing or removing staff:

  • Click the Edit icon on any staff card to update details or reset their password.
  • Click the Delete icon to remove the account. Classes assigned to a deleted coach should be reassigned first.

4.4 Managing Customers & Children

Page: power2adapt.online/admin/customers  Admin

This page shows all registered parent accounts and their associated children.

  • Search customers by name or email using the search bar.
  • View child profiles including date of birth, grade, medical notes, and emergency contact.
  • View enrolment history for each child — current and past programs.
  • Edit customer details — update contact information or correct data entry errors.

4.5 Athlete Performance Manager

See Section 3.3 for full details. Admins have all the same capabilities as coaches, plus they can add new athlete profiles independently of parent accounts.

4.6 Term & Pricing Configuration

Page: power2adapt.online/admin/term-config  Admin

This is where term dates, pricing, and public holidays are configured. All class pricing is calculated dynamically from these settings.

Creating a term configuration:

  1. Click + New Term.
  2. Select the term (Term 1–4), year, give it a name (e.g. "Term 2 2026"), and set the start date and end date.
  3. Enter the number of teaching weeks (the system will cross-check against public holidays).
  4. Set the price per week (e.g. $30.00) and GST rate (default 10%).
  5. Optionally set enrolment open and close dates.
  6. Click Create Term.

Adding public holidays / school holidays:

  1. Find the term in the list and click the Holidays button.
  2. Click + Add Holiday, enter the date, name (e.g. "King's Birthday"), and type (Public Holiday or School Holiday).
  3. The holiday is subtracted from teaching weeks and the price recalculates automatically.
ℹ️
Venue-specific school holiday dates (e.g. Peninsula Grammar finishing earlier than other venues) can be added as separate holiday entries. The pricing engine accounts for all listed holidays when calculating the term fee.

  3. Review the recipient count and estimated cost.
  4. Click Send SMS. Messages are delivered within seconds.
⚠️
Automated SMS messages are also sent for: new enrolments, payment confirmations, waitlist movements, application confirmations, and term renewal reminders. These do not require any manual action.

Common Questions & Answers

A parent says they can't log in — what do I do?

Check their email address in Manage Customers. Confirm it matches what they registered with. If needed, an admin can update their account details. For password resets, the user can use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen, or an admin can update their details.

A class shows as full but a spot has opened — how does the waitlist work?

When an enrolled student withdraws (admin removes enrolment), the system detects the capacity freed up. The next person on the waitlist receives an automatic SMS. An admin should also manually confirm with the family if needed.

How do I add a new venue?

Venues are managed through the backend. Contact the system administrator (business owner) to add a new venue — it requires a short technical update to the database.

A parent didn't receive their SMS confirmation — what should I check?

Confirm their mobile number in Manage Customers. It must be a valid Australian number: mobile starting with 04, or a landline, with or without the +61 country code prefix. International numbers are not supported.

How do I know if a payment went through?

Check the enrolment in Manage Customers or the Admin Dashboard. The enrolment status will show Active if payment was successful, or Pending Payment if it hasn't been completed yet. All successful payments are also listed in the Analytics page revenue section.

Can I export data for reporting?

Survey responses are automatically synced to Google Sheets. For other data (enrolments, payments, customers), use the Analytics dashboard for on-screen reporting. For bulk exports, contact the system administrator.
