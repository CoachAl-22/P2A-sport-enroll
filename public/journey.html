Each Week

Learn. Do. Reflect.

Every training week follows the same simple loop — about 15 minutes of app time, with the "Do" happening out on the track.

STEP 1 — LEARN

One big idea

A short, age-pitched concept: what creates speed, why landings matter, how focus works. Read it before training.

+10 XP
STEP 2 — DO

The challenge

A physical challenge tied to that week's session — squat jumps, fast starts, relay exchanges — completed at training.

+10 XP & badges
STEP 3 — REFLECT

Think it through

A guided journal prompt. Coach Al reads every reflection — it's where the real development happens.

+15 XP

Common Questions

Quick answers

Does the app cost anything?

No — it's included with every Power2ADAPT term program enrolment.

How does my child get a login?

Coach Al creates every account personally and hands the login to you at training. There's no public sign-up — that's deliberate, for the kids' safety.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. Each athlete can only see their own journey, only their coach can see their reflections, and there are no social feeds, ads, or contact with other users.

What devices does it work on?

Any iPhone, iPad, Android phone or tablet, and any computer browser. Adding it to the home screen makes it work like a normal app, including notifications.

How much screen time is it?

About 15 minutes a week — read one concept, mark off the challenge done at training, and write a short reflection.

Forgot the password?

Ask Coach Al at training or send a message — he can reset it in under a minute.
